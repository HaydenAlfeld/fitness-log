# Nutrition Plan

## Daily Calorie Target
3,100 calories

## Protein Goal
200–220 grams per day

## Sample Meal Plan

### Breakfast
- 4 eggs
- Oatmeal
- Banana
- Milk

### Lunch
- Grilled chicken
- Rice
- Vegetables

### Dinner
- Lean ground beef
- Sweet potatoes
- Salad

### Snacks
- Protein shake
- Greek yogurt
- Peanut butter sandwich

## Hydration
Minimum 1 gallon of water per day.

## Notes
Prioritize protein intake and whole foods.
Limit processed foods.
