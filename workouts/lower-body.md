# Lower Body Workout Plan

## Warm-Up
- 5 minutes bike ride
- Hip mobility drills
- Bodyweight squats (2 x 15)

## Main Lifts
- Back Squat – 4 x 5
- Romanian Deadlift – 3 x 8
- Walking Lunges – 3 x 10 each leg

## Accessory Work
- Leg Curl Machine – 3 x 12
- Calf Raises – 4 x 15
- Core Work (Planks) – 3 x 45 seconds

## Notes
Maintain proper depth on squats and controlled descent.
