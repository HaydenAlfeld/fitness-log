# Upper Body Workout Plan

## Warm-Up
- 5 minutes light cardio
- Shoulder mobility drills
- Band pull-aparts (3 x 15)

## Main Lifts
- Bench Press – 4 x 5
- Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 x 8
- Overhead Press – 3 x 6
- Barbell Rows – 4 x 6

## Accessory Work
- Lat Pulldowns – 3 x 10
- Dumbbell Curls – 3 x 12
- Tricep Pushdowns – 3 x 12
- face pulls - 3 x 15

## Notes
Focus on progressive overload and controlled tempo.
