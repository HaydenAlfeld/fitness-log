# Conditioning Routine

## Circuit (Repeat 4 Rounds)
- 10 Burpees
- 15 Kettlebell Swings
- 20 Jump Squats
- 200m Sprint

Rest: 90 seconds between rounds

## Finisher
- Battle Ropes – 3 x 30 seconds
- Core Circuit (Sit-ups, Russian Twists)

## Notes
Goal is intensity and consistency, not maximum weight.
